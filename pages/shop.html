Nestled in the heart of South France, Coffee Roastery began as a humble family venture in 1989, inspired by a shared love for the art of coffee. What started as a small café in a charming village quickly grew into a renowned roastery, celebrated for its dedication to sourcing the finest beans and crafting exceptional blends. From the rolling coffee plantations of Ethiopia to the vibrant farms of Colombia, we partner directly with farmers to ensure sustainable practices and unparalleled quality. Each cup tells a story of tradition, passion, and a relentless pursuit of perfection. Whether you're savoring a bold espresso or a smooth latte, you're tasting decades of expertise and a commitment to the timeless ritual of coffee. Welcome to the Coffee Roastery family—where every sip feels like home.
